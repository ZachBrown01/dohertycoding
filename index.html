CLUB INFO

Welcome to the 2022-23 Doherty coding club! Our club is dedicated not only to honing your coding abilities but also to developing critical skills such as problem solving, creativity, and mental toughness. It is our strong belief that anyone, with the right dedication and fortitude, can become a great programmer. As the world becomes more technologically advanced and dependent on computing power, coding is a skill that helps you understand these emergent technologies, while giving you the power to change them yourself. As with any learned skill - it will be hard, it will be frustating, and you will want to quit. Know that it'll all be worth it when you finish each problem and create beautiful, functional code. You are not alone, and you will not be alone. We are a community, and we will help you in your struggles, and we will cherish your successes. Help is everywhere, and the answers are there for those who want it. Happy coding!
